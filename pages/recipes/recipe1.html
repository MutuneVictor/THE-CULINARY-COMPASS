Recipe 1: Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta

This dish is a classic for a reason. It's creamy, flavorful, and easy to make. The fresh basil gives it a bright and herbaceous flavor, while the tomatoes add a touch of sweetness and acidity.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pasta (such as penne, fusilli, or rotini)
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, in a large saucepan, heat the crushed tomatoes over medium heat.Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, or until thickened.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and basil.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce. Toss to coat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

This Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta is so satisfying and comforting. The sauce is rich and creamy, and the basil adds a wonderful freshness. It's a great dish to make for a weeknight meal or a casual dinner party.
