Recipe 2: One-Pan Lemon Herb Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

This dish is a healthy and flavorful way to enjoy salmon. The salmon is baked with lemon, herbs, and vegetables, so it's packed with nutrients and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound salmon fillet
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and Brussels sprouts), cut into bite-sized pieces

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the vegetables out on a baking sheet.
  4. Place the salmon fillet on top of the vegetables.
  5. Drizzle the salmon with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, thyme, and oregano.
  6. Arrange the lemon slices on top of the salmon.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

This One-Pan Lemon Herb Salmon with Roasted Vegetables is a delicious and nutritious meal. The salmon is cooked to perfection, and the vegetables are tender and flavorful. It's a great dish to make for a healthy and satisfying dinner.
