Recipe 3: Easy Chicken Pad Thai

This dish is a quick and easy way to make Pad Thai at home. It's made with rice noodles, chicken, eggs, and a flavorful Pad Thai sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound rice noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into strips
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup Pad Thai sauce
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to package directions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Push the chicken to one side of the skillet and add the eggs. Scramble the eggs.
  5. Stir in the cooked rice noodles, Pad Thai sauce, fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce is heated through.
  7. Garnish with peanuts and scallions.

This Easy Chicken Pad Thai is a flavorful and satisfying dish. The Pad Thai sauce is sweet, sour, and salty, and the chicken is tender and juicy. It's a great dish to make for a quick and easy weeknight meal.
